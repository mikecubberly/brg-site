BRG Industries

Verticals We Run LinkedIn Outbound For

Bottle Rocket Growth runs LinkedIn outbound campaigns across multiple B2B verticals, with deeper expertise in industries where signal-based outbound outperforms generic prospecting. Every vertical page below covers the signals we source, the ICP patterns we see, and the messaging that works.

Logistics

Freight & Logistics Brokerages

Signal-based outbound for freight brokerages, 3PLs, and logistics companies. FMCSA filings, TMS technographics, capacity signals.
Technology

IT Managed Service Providers

LinkedIn outbound for MSPs, MSSPs, and IT consultancies. Compliance triggers, security incident signals, vertical specialization.
Insurance

Commercial Insurance & MGAs

Outbound for MGAs, commercial brokers, and wholesale platforms. State licensing signals, appointment changes, renewal-cycle timing.

Other Verticals We Serve

BRG also runs campaigns in industrial distribution, commercial printing and packaging, building materials and construction supply, specialized staffing, TIC and testing firms, equipment rental, waste management, technology consultancies, and venture capital and private equity firms.
