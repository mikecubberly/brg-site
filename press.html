Company Boilerplate

Bottle Rocket Growth (BRG) is a Chicago-based LinkedIn outbound agency and go-to-market consultancy for B2B startups. Founded in 2024 by Mike Cubberly, BRG runs AI-driven outbound campaigns using borrowed LinkedIn sender profiles, signal-based list building, and sequenced outreach via HeyReach. Services include ICP definition, top-of-funnel messaging, targeted contact list building, and full-funnel outbound orchestration. BRG is part of the Bottle Rocket family, which includes Bottle Rocket Talent (GTM talent placement), Bottle Rocket Racing (motorsports sponsorship), and Leasr (automotive leasing marketplace).

Short Description (50 words)

Bottle Rocket Growth is a Chicago-based LinkedIn outbound agency that runs signal-based campaigns on HeyReach for B2B startups. Founded in 2024 by Mike Cubberly, BRG specializes in ICP definition, signal-based prospect lists, and sequenced LinkedIn outreach delivered as Done-For-You, Infrastructure-On-You, or Lite Advisory engagements.

Founder Bio

Mike Cubberly is the founder of Bottle Rocket Growth. For over a decade, Mike has helped B2B tech companies build, grow, and scale go-to-market teams. Prior to launching BRG in 2024, Mike held GTM roles at Verkada, Glencoco, Primer, and RevGenius. He is based in Chicago.
