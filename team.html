The BRG Team

Who Runs Bottle Rocket Growth

Bottle Rocket Growth operates as a founder-led agency with a rotating network of specialist contractors. The structure is intentional. Fixed overhead stays small. Specialist capability scales up and down with engagement demand.

Mike Cubberly, Founder

MC
Mike Cubberly
Founder · Chicago, IL · LinkedIn · GitHub

Mike Cubberly founded Bottle Rocket Growth in 2024 after more than a decade building and scaling go-to-market teams at B2B tech companies. Prior roles span Verkada (cloud physical security), Glencoco (sales development marketplace), Primer (B2B signal and targeting), and RevGenius (revenue professionals community).

At BRG, Mike owns strategy, ICP workshops, messaging development, and client relationship management. For DFY and IOY engagements, Mike runs the kickoff and tracks campaign performance weekly with each client.

His focus is helping organizations treat growth as a tangible milestone through disciplined outbound, signal-based prospecting, and messaging that prioritizes prospect context over generic automation.

Specialist Network

BRG maintains a network of specialist contractors who contribute to engagements based on scope and vertical. Roles include:

  • List builders: Apollo, Clay, and vertical-specific data specialists for signal-based prospect sourcing.
  • LinkedIn sender operators: Trained operators managing borrowed sender profiles across DFY engagements.
  • Copywriters: Outbound messaging specialists with vertical experience in logistics, insurance, MSP, and SaaS.
  • Engineers: For custom tool builds including the BRG ICP Engine, Campaign Studio, and EventEnricher.

All specialists are under NDA. Client data never leaves the BRG operational stack without explicit permission.

The Broader Family

Each Bottle Rocket brand runs independently with its own leadership:

  • Bottle Rocket Talent: GTM talent placement. Reach out via the main contact for introductions.
  • Bottle Rocket Racing: Motorsports sponsorship. Visit bottlerocketracing.com.
  • Leasr: Automotive leasing marketplace. Visit leasr.io.

Hiring

BRG occasionally hires specialist contractors with deep vertical GTM experience or advanced technical skills in Clay, HeyReach, and Apollo workflows. Send a note to mike@bottlerocketgrowth.com if interested.
